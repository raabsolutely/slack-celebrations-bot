# 🎉 Celebrations Bot Demo

An interactive prototype demonstrating how the Celebrations Bot works in Slack.

## What is Celebrations Bot?

A Slack bot that encourages teams to share weekly wins.

**How it works:**
- Every Friday at 3 PM, team members receive a friendly DM
- One click to share a win with the team
- Celebrations are posted to #celebrations for everyone to see
- Users can skip any week - no pressure

## View the Demo

**[→ View Live Demo](https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/celebrations-bot-demo)**

Or open `index.html` locally in your browser.

## Permissions Required

The bot needs these Slack permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| `channels:read` | See who's in #celebrations |
| `chat:write` | Post celebration messages |
| `im:write` | Send Friday prompts via DM |
| `users:read` | Identify team members |

## Privacy & Security

✅ Does NOT read any channel messages
✅ Does NOT access private channels or DMs
✅ Does NOT store data externally
✅ Does NOT share information outside Slack
